# Code Of Conduct

Vellis should be a practical, respectful engineering space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, factual, and constructive.
- Critique designs and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the software and its documentation.
- Respect maintainers' decisions about component boundaries and accepted specs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Ignoring maintainer requests to stop disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, block participation, or take other action needed to keep the project workable. Report conduct issues to the maintainer at <mloumagnuson@gmail.com>.
