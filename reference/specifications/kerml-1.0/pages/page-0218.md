/behavior : Behavior [0..*] {subsets type, ordered}

The Behaviors     that type thisStep.

/parameter : Feature [0..*] {redefines directedFeature, ordered}

The parameters     of thisStep, which are defined as its  directedFeatures, whose values are passed into and/or
out of a performance of the  Step.
