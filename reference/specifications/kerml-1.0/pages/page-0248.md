8.3.4.9.3 FlowEnd

Description

A FlowEnd   is aFeature    that is one of theconnectorEnds     giving the source   or target   of aFlow. For  Flows
typed by FlowTransfer     or its specializations,FlowEnds   must have exactly one   ownedFeature, which redefines
Transfer::source::sourceOutput            orTransfer::target::targetInput            and redefines the corresponding
feature of therelatedElement      for its end.
