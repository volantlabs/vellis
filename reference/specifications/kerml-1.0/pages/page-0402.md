9.3.3.2.8  OrderedMap

Element

DataType

Description

An  OrderedMap     is a variable size  Map  that maintains ordering of its elements.

The ordering may be by key of the      KeyValuePair       elements, or by order of construction, or any other method. The
essential aspect is that ordering is maintained and guaranteed across accesses to the        OrderedMap.
