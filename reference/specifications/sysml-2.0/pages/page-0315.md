8.3.9.3 OccurrenceDefinition

Description

An OccurrenceDefinition          is aDefinition     of a Class  of individuals that have an independent life over time
and potentially an extent over space. This includes both structural things and behaviors that act on such structures. If
isIndividual     is true, then theOccurrenceDefinition          is constrained to have (at most) a single instance that is
the entire life of a single individual.
