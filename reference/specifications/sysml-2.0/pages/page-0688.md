engine6Cyl         has an attribute     diameterthat is also a variation point, with two variants forsmallDiameter                                  and
largeDiameter. There are also two choices for thetransmission                                 and a   sunroof      is optional. The choice of a
selected variant at one variation point can constrain the available choices at another variation point. For this
example, the choices are constrained to bea 4 cylinder engine with a                         manual transmission or a 6 cylinder engine
with an automatic transmission.
