8.3.22.4 ObjectiveMembership

Description

An ObjectiveMembership        is aFeatureMembership        that indicates that itsownedObjectiveRequirement         is
the objectiveRequirementUsage       for itsowningType, which must be a     CaseDefinition      or CaseUsage.

General Classes

FeatureMembership

Attributes

/ownedObjectiveRequirement : RequirementUsage {redefines ownedMemberFeature}

The RequirementUsage that is the   ownedMemberFeature        of this RequirementUsage.

Operations

None.
